**Design System (tokens)**

* **Palette**
  `--brand-emerald-600:#10B981; --brand-teal-500:#06B6D4; --accent-purple-500:#7C3AED; --error-600:#DC2626; --warning-600:#D97706; --success-600:#16A34A; --gray-900:#0F172A; --gray-700:#334155; --gray-100:#F1F5F9;`

* **Typography**
  H1 28/36 • H2 22/28 • H3 18/24 • Body 14/20 • Small 12/18.
  Font: Inter/Manrope; numeric tabular for KPIs.

  xs 6 • sm 10 • md 14 • lg 20; shadows: `e1/e2/e3` with subtle ambient.
* **Spacing/Grid**
  8-pt scale: 4/8/12/16/20/24/32/40/64; container max 1200.
* **Motion**
  120ms/180ms ease-out; reduced-motion safe.

## Tiny Style Guide (use immediately)

* **Primary** `#10B981` (emerald-600), hover `#059669`; **Gradient** teal-500 `#06B6D4` → emerald-600.
* **Text**: on light `#0F172A`, on dark `#F8FAFC`.
* **Surface**: light `#FFFFFF`, dark `#0B1220`; cards `#0F1626`.
* **Error/Warning/Success**: `#DC2626` / `#D97706` / `#16A34A`.
* **Radius**: 14 (cards), 10 (inputs), 20 (prominent CTAs).
* **Focus ring**: 2px `#06B6D4` outside border; keyboard only.

* Axe score ≥ 98; all text/icon contrast AA.
* Forms: inline validation, disabled CTA reason present.
* No z-index overlaps; selects and popovers never cover other inputs.
* Stats formatted human-readably (no long decimals, friendly units).
* Visual tests: light/dark/high-contrast parity.
* Brand applied: logo, icons, illustrations—no placeholders.
